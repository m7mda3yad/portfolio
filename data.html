DalilyMap - Mobile Application & Admin Panel

DalilyMap is a mobile application and admin panel designed to help users find various local services within their area, including teachers, workers, restaurants, schools, and all activities. Users can browse services, view details and offers, communicate with providers, and see user ratings. Providers can add their services, promote them through sponsored ads or send direct messages to users.

Key Features

  • Locate and explore local services such as teachers, workers, restaurants, schools, etc.
  • View service details, offers, and communication methods
  • User rating and feedback system
  • Providers can add and manage their services
  • Admin panel for full control over the platform
  • Send notifications to users
